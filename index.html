USDTZ BEP20

USD-Pegged Token on BNB Chain

Overview

USDTZ is a BEP20 token deployed on BNB Chain, designed to maintain a 1:1 peg with the US Dollar through on-chain liquidity mechanisms.

The project focuses on transparency, simplicity, and verifiable smart contracts.

Peg Mechanism

USDTZ is designed to maintain a 1:1 value with the US Dollar through on-chain liquidity mechanisms.

The peg is supported by decentralized liquidity pools, where USDTZ can be exchanged directly against USD-denominated stable assets.

When the market price deviates from 1 USD, arbitrage opportunities naturally incentivize market participants to buy or sell USDTZ on decentralized exchanges, helping restore the peg.

All liquidity and price data are fully transparent and verifiable on-chain.

Transparency

All smart contracts are open-source and publicly available.

Liquidity is managed on-chain and visible through decentralized exchanges and CoinGecko Terminal.
